About Bapatla Engineering College

Bapatla Engineering College (BEC) is a private, autonomous engineering college established in 1981 by the Bapatla Education Society. Located in Bapatla, the college has been a leader in technical education for over four decades, offering a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs. It is known for its strong academic curriculum, modern infrastructure, and a focus on research and innovation.

The CSE department is a hub for innovation, focusing on the latest technologies in software and computer systems.

  • Web Development: HTML, CSS, JavaScript

ECE focuses on the design, development, and maintenance of electronic equipment and communication systems.

Placements:

Top Recruiters: Bosch, Tech Mahindra, Wipro

Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE)

EEE covers power systems, control systems, and electronics, with a focus on both electrical and electronic applications.

Placements:

Placement Rate: 75%-80% (for M.Tech)

Technologies Used:

  • Control Systems: PLC, PID controllers

  • Renewable Energy: Solar and wind power technologies
